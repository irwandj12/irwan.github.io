Transition Question

Transition questions are types of questions that are used to connect or move the topic of conversation from one thing to another. Example : Now, let's turn our attention to B. What are your thoughts on that?"

Implied Detail Question

Implied detail questions are a type of question that indirectly implies additional information or details are expected in the answer. Example 1. Would you like some help?" (Implied: I am ready to help if you need it.)

Locating pronoun references

Locating pronoun references and specific information involves the ability to identify pronouns (Person, thing, or place pronouns) in a text and locate relevant or specific information associated with those pronouns. Examples of personal pronouns include "I", "you", or "they", while examples of nouns include "it", "this", or "them".
